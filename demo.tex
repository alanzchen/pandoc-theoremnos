\begin{thm}[Pythagorean theorem]\label{thm:pythagorean} 

For a right triangle, if \(c\) denotes the length of the hypotenuse and
\(a\) and \(b\) denote the lengths of the other two sides, we have

\end{thm}

\[a^2 + b^2 = c^2\]

\Cref{thm:pythagorean} is very popular and is unrelated to
\cref{dfn:good}.
